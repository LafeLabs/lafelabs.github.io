About

My name is Lafe. I studied math, physics, and technology for 20 years(Berkeley BA, 1998 math and physics, Yale applied physics PhD 2006), then started two companies, and after a few years concluded that capitalism is in contradiction with my goals for science and technology and now I build free things, where I try to use as strict a definition of "free" as possible. Details on what I mean by "free" can be found in the Trash Magic Manifesto.
