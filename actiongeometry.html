Action Geometry

Action Geometry is a set of 5 constructions designed to teach the practice of a practical form of geometry that can be used for construction of technology and for everyday life.

C1: First Construction

Shapes in the Plane

C2: Second Construction

Platonic Solids

C3: Third Construction

ArtBox, Practical Cardboard and Duct Tape Construction

C4: Fourth Construction

Five Elementals

C5: Fifth Construction

Action Geography and Treasure Maps
